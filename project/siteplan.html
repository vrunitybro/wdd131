VR Unity Portfolio Site Plan

Site Name

VRUnityPortfolio

This name represents a portfolio site focused on showcasing my Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR), and Augmented Reality (AR) projects, aiming to engage potential customers or employers interested in immersive experiences. Domain availability (optional): vrunityportfolio.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a comprehensive portfolio that highlights my VR, MR, and AR projects. The site will contain information about each project, including demos, videos, and descriptions to showcase skills and experiences. The site will also provide a contact page for potential collaborations or job offers.

Scenarios

  • What kind of projects has the developer worked on in Mixed Reality?
  • How can I contact the developer for collaboration or job opportunities?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme includes:

  • Blue: #0033cc - used for headings and links.
  • White: #ffffff - used for the background.
  • Purple: #800080 - used as accent colors for buttons and borders.

Typography

Fonts used:

  • Header Font: Roboto, used for all headings.
  • Body Font: Open Sans, used for paragraph text.
